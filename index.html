open-feeder

don't write a cron job to refresh feeds.

open-feeder is a public service. It subscribes to all RSS/Atom feeds throughout the internet. It constantly parses, archives and serves the contents, and notifies the world with the updating.

News, blogs, podcasts, video channels and so on, feeds are the future of a free and open internet.

free and open

Free for all. No authentication. Open-sourced and self-hostable.

simple yet powerful

A dead simple RESTful API.

Hooks to notify updates.

try it

check if a feed exists
$ curl https://api.open-feeder.com/feeds\
  ?url="https://news.un.org/feed/subscribe/en/news/all/rss.xml"
add a feed
$ curl https://api.open-feeder.com/feeds \
  -d url="https://news.un.org/feed/subscribe/en/news/all/rss.xml"
fetch entries
$ curl https://api.open-feeder.com/entries?feed_url="https://news.un.org/feed/subscribe/en/news/all/rss.xml"
